# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled by the **Khmer En Ru dictionary** mobile application ("the App").

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
The App is designed to protect your privacy.
- **No Personal Data:** The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.
- **Offline Functionality:** The App primarily operates offline. It does not require you to create an account or log in.
- **No External Tracking:** The App does not use third-party analytics or tracking tools that collect data about your usage.

## 2. Permissions
The App may request certain permissions to function correctly, such as:
- **Storage/Media:** To store and access dictionary databases on your device.
- **Network Access:** Only if required for checking updates or downloading additional dictionary data (if applicable).

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not contain advertisements and does not integrate with third-party advertising or data-sharing services.

## 4. Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for children to use.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at srghma@gmail.com.
